Why it's different

Most AI plugins guess.
This one checks.

Every answer about a product, price, or your store is read from WooCommerce through real tools, then shown to the shopper as cards from trusted data, never paraphrased into new claims.

Fahad AI Grounded

Calls a real tool, reads the live value, shows it as a card. If there's nothing to report, it says so.

"The Nordic Mug Set is on sale, $49.99 → $34.99."
verified against your products

Typical bot Hallucinated

Pattern-matches a plausible answer from training data and presents made-up specifics as fact.

"Yes, it's 30% off and only 2 left, order now!"
— none of that was true
Capabilities

A full storefront concierge

// search

Grounded product search

Finds products by need, category, price or what's on sale, and renders them as rich cards with photo, price and stock.

// cart

Real cart actions

Adds to cart, picks variations and hands off to checkout, with confirmations that reflect the actual cart result, never a narrated guess.

// wallet

Wallet-aware

With WalletPro installed, it reads a signed-in shopper's real store credit and can shop within their balance.

// i18n

Multilingual + voice

Replies in the shopper's language, with optional speech-to-text and spoken answers via the browser's voice APIs.

// rag

Semantic search

Optional embeddings and vector search understand intent ("shoes for flat feet") beyond literal keyword matches.

// channels

WhatsApp ready

The same grounded assistant answers on a WhatsApp business number, not just the web widget.

// admin

Merchant copilot

Admin-only, capability-gated endpoints surface real sales, refund and review data, and propose sales, never auto-changing anything.

// agents

Agent-ready

Read-only /agent endpoints and an llms.txt let external AI agents discover and shop your store safely.

// trust

Honest by design

No fabricated scarcity, no pushing over-budget items, PII kept out of model context. The trust rules are non-overridable.

How to use

Live on your store in minutes.

1

Download

Grab the latest release from GitHub. It's free and GPL-licensed, no signup.

2

Install & activate

In WordPress, go to Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin, choose the zip, and activate. WooCommerce must be active.

3

Add your API key

Open WooCommerce → Fahad AI, pick your provider, and paste your key. You pay the model provider directly.
